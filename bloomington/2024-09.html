Kids Sing

Tuesday September 03 - 06:30 PM to 07:15 PM

Location: The Davis Academy of Music,336 South Walnut Street, Bloomington, IN 47401, USA

Join our academy in a low pressure live group class every Tuesday from 6:30 to 7:15 p.m. At Kids Sing, you’ll learn to sing your favorite Disney, Pop and Music Theatre songs while enjoying in a fun, active group setting. Rotating topics include proper vocal technique and warm-ups, music theatre, opera, country, recording techniques, and more. No prior musical skills are necessary to enjoy this group course.
